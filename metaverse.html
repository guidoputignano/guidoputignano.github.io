Key Government Initiatives

Strategic programs driving metaverse and VR adoption

Vision 2030

National transformation plan to diversify economy and establish Saudi Arabia as a global digital leader. Target: 19.4% digital economy contribution to GDP by 2025.

  • Economic diversification away from oil
  • Digital infrastructure development
  • Innovation and entrepreneurship support
  • Global technology partnerships

I.D.E.A Initiative

Immersive Digital Environments and Assets Initiative - Creating a vibrant immersive technology sector through collaboration between MCIT and TONOMUS.

  • AR/VR ecosystem development
  • Virtual collaboration spaces
  • Industrial metaverse applications
  • 15+ tech partners onboarded

NEOM Project

$500 billion futuristic mega-city with digital twin in the metaverse. Leading cognitive technology and immersive experiences development.

  • Digital twin technology integration
  • Smart city infrastructure
  • 5G and beyond connectivity
  • The Line - 170km linear city

National AI Strategy

Led by SDAIA, integrating AI with metaverse technologies to position KSA as regional AI hub with focus on ethical AI development.

  • AI-powered immersive experiences
  • Data infrastructure development
  • Research and innovation centers
  • International AI partnerships
